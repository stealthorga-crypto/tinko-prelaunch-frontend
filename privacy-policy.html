TINKO — PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: 29 Nov 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Tinko, a product of Blocks & Loops Technologies Pvt Ltd, collects, uses, and protects your data.

2. What Data We Collect

Merchant Data (You)

  • Name, business details
  • Email and phone number
  • Login details (securely hashed)
  • API keys that you provide voluntarily
  • Configuration settings

Transaction Metadata (Optional/When Enabled)

  • Transaction ID/reference
  • Amount
  • Status (failed/success/pending)
  • Recovery status
We do NOT collect card numbers, UPI PINs, CVV, passwords, OTPs, or any sensitive cardholder data.

3. Customer Data (Your Customers)

We may process:

  • Name
  • Contact (email/phone) for recovery communication
  • Transaction reference

We never contact your customers without your explicit action or configuration. We do not store or access payment method details.

4. How We Use Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide the Tinko service
  • Send recovery notifications
  • Improve product features
  • Monitor performance and prevent fraud
  • Provide support

5. How We Protect Your Data

We use:

  • Encryption (in transit & at rest)
  • Secure databases
  • Access controls
  • Monitoring systems

We never sell your data.

6. Data Sharing

We only share data with trusted service providers, such as:

  • SMS providers
  • Email providers
  • WhatsApp business API
  • Hosting providers

These vendors only process data necessary to run the service.

7. Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies for analytics and improving user experience. No targeted ads, no third-party marketing trackers.

8. Data Retention

We keep data only as long as needed for business operations or as required by law. You can request deletion anytime.

9. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access your data
  • Update incorrect info
  • Request deletion
  • Request export of your data

Email: info@blocksandloops.com

10. Children's Data

Tinko is not intended for minors under 18. We do not knowingly collect such data.

11. Updates to This Policy

We may update the Privacy Policy. Major updates will be communicated.
